# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2026
**Last Updated:** January 2026

Strong and Savage Nutrition ("we," "us," or "our") operates a meal ordering platform that connects our meal preparation business with clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including our SMS notification program.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you become a client of Strong and Savage Nutrition, we may collect:

- **Contact Information:** Name, phone number, email address
- **Payment Information:** Venmo handle (we do not process or store credit card information)
- **Order Information:** Meal selections, order history, preferences

### Automatically Collected Information

When you interact with our SMS program, we collect:

- **Phone Number:** In E.164 international format
- **SMS Consent Status:** Whether you have opted in or out of SMS notifications
- **Consent Timestamps:** Date and time of opt-in and opt-out actions
- **Message Logs:** Records of SMS messages sent and received

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Services:** Process your meal orders and deliver notifications
- **Send SMS Notifications:** Alert you about new menus, order confirmations, and service updates (with your consent)
- **Send Email Communications:** Provide order information and menu updates
- **Process Payments:** Generate Venmo payment requests for your orders
- **Improve Services:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
- **Comply with Legal Obligations:** Maintain records as required by law

---

## SMS Program Details

### Message Types

With your consent, we send SMS messages including:

- New weekly menu availability notifications
- Order confirmation messages
- Important service announcements

### Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on our menu schedule. Typically, you will receive 1-3 messages per week during active ordering periods.

### Opt-In and Opt-Out

- **Opt-In:** You must provide express consent by replying YES, START, or similar affirmative keywords to our opt-in request
- **Opt-Out:** Reply STOP, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, END, or QUIT to any message to unsubscribe
- **Help:** Reply HELP to receive program information and support contact

### Costs

Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan. We do not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier may.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Secure database storage with access controls
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for data transmission
- Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security reviews and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you remain an active client and for a reasonable period thereafter for legal and business purposes. Specifically:

- **Account Information:** Retained while you are an active client
- **Order History:** Retained for accounting and tax purposes
- **SMS Consent Records:** Retained for compliance documentation
- **Opt-Out Records:** Retained indefinitely to honor your preferences

You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
